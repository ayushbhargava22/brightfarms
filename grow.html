No Pesticide Ever

Bright Farms ensures there are no pesticide, herbicides, or fungicides used on our greens. Even organic can't say the same.

No Rising Required

Our process is simple. We start with non-GMO seeds and water, add the power of sunshine, and voila! Perfect produce. Growing in a greenhouse allows for control over everything in and around the greens, so you can trust your salad is ready to eat, right out of the box.

Clean and Green

BrightFarms greenhouses are hydroponic. This means our crops are grown in water instead of soil. Our method allows us to use 80% less water, 90% less land, and 95% less shipping fuel than long-distance field grown produce. On top of being more sustainable, the greenhouse gives the plants a happy, sunny, 72 degree home, year-round, anywhere in the US, even in the winter. So local is always available.
